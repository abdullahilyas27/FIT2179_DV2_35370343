# Beneath the Surface

**FIT2179 Data Visualisation II — Monash University · 2026**
**By Abdullah Ilyas Siddiqui · ID 35370343**

## Topic

The visualisation revolves around a headline Australians meet every summer: *Shark Incidents are Rising*. The visualisation argues that the headline may be inaccurately giving the notion that the risk of facing a shark has risen. It does so by tracing through the per-capita incident rate to gauge risk, identifying the two driving forces behind the higher incident rate, and then providing a detailed breakdown on shark incidents that accurately and impartially portrays the numbers.
